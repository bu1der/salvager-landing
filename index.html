Salvager is a marketplace for users to buy and sell salvage and junk cars, within a network of junkyards, towing companies, and car dealers.

Now serving the Denver, CO metro area
How it works

Sell your junk car. Get the best offer.

01

List Your Car

Snap a few photos, enter the make and model, and tell us where the car is. Listing takes about two minutes.

02

Get Bids

Local junkyards, towing companies, and dealers receive your listing and compete to buy your car.

03

Get Paid

Accept the best offer. The buyer arranges pickup and pays you on the spot. Salvager takes a small percentage of the final sale.
